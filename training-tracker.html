What is ACWR?

The Acute:Chronic Workload Ratio compares your last 7 days of training load (acute) against your rolling 4-week average (chronic). Load = RPE × session duration in minutes. Log your RPE to populate this chart.

Log RPE on your sessions to see your ACWR.
